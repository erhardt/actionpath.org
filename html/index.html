Action Path

Presentation at Knight-MIT Civic Media Conference, June 2014.
Imagine you are in Kendall Square, Cambridge and you get a push notification from your smartphone.
Action Path asks you to answer a one-question survey about a proposed development on the block.
While waiting for the bus, you can learn about an issue, provided feedback on it, and subscribed to updates about it.
Action Path employs Jane Jacobs' idea of routine monitoring of the city and aggregates it for impact through mobile tech.
